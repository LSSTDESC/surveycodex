    galcheat.survey¶

    Survey dataclass ¶

    A dataclass for storing the parameters of a survey

    available_filters: List[str] dataclass-field ¶

    The list of survey filters

    description: str dataclass-field ¶

    The survey description with telescope/instrument information

    effective_area: Quantity dataclass-field ¶

    The survey instrument effective area on the sky computed from the obscuration

    gain: Quantity dataclass-field ¶

    The gain in electron/ADU

    mirror_diameter: Quantity dataclass-field ¶

    The mirror diameter

    name: str dataclass-field ¶

    The survey name

    obscuration: Quantity dataclass-field ¶

    The total obscuration created by the instrument pieces

    pixel_scale: Quantity dataclass-field ¶

    The pixel scale of the survey

    references: Dict[str, Dict[str, str]] dataclass-field ¶

    Dictionary of references for each parameter specified in galcheat

    zeropoint_airmass: Quantity dataclass-field ¶

    The zeropoint airmass
